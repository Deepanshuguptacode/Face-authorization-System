Why Threshold 0.3 Works Better:

The mean similarity for same-person pairs is ~0.53-0.54, while different-person pairs average ~0.01. Threshold 0.3 sits comfortably between these values, catching all imposters while accepting most legitimate users. Threshold 0.6 is above the mean for legitimate users, causing excessive rejections without any security benefit.
